Movie review - Premante Maade
Premante Maade
Credits

Jeevi rating: 0.5/5
Punchline: avoidable
Genre: Love
Can kids watch this film? Yes (no vulgarity and no violence)
Banner: Sri God Gift Films
Cast: Vinay Babu, Reena, Rashmi, Amar, Vizag Prasad, Mallikharjuna Rao, AVS, LB Sreeram, Gowtam Raju, MS Narayana, Ramya Sri, Duvvasi Mohan, Rajitha, Jr. Relangi, Ram Lakshman, Telangana Sakuntala
Music: MM Srilekha
Lyrics: Sirivennela, Sahiti, Sweta Nagu
Stunts: Ram Lakshman
Cinematography: Ram Prasad
Producer: D.M.L. Yadav
Story - Screenplay - direction: L Vemu
Release date: 2nd April 2004
Review

Story

Vinay (Vinay Babu) and his three friends are orphans. They run 'A-Z sevices' that cater to petty needs of the colony they live in. Soumya (Reena) is the daughter of the colony's president Tapi Dharma Rao (Vizag Prasad). Vinay and Soumya fall in love. Tapi Dharma Rao rejects their proposal and gets Vinay beaten up. The rest of the film is all about how Vinay succeeds in winning the confidence of Tapi Dharma Rao.

Artists Performance

Vinay Babu: When I entered the theater, the film is already started and the first song is being screened. It's colony song where everybody is dancing. I had a tough time in deciding who the hero is. Vinay Babu does not have minimum screen presence. His body language is negative and comical at times.

Others: Reena is good as heroine. She has got Telugu looks. Vizag Prasad used Nellore accent, which appeared inconsistent to him. The comedy track by AVS, Mallikharjuna Rao, LB Sriram and Gowtam Raju is tasteless.

Technical Departments:

Story - Screenplay - direction: There is no story for the first two hours of the film. When you realize that the story has just started, you would be greeted with 'Subham' card. Screenplay of the film is erratic. Direction has nothing to write home about.

Other departments: Only plus point of the film is music MM Srilekha. She is one girl who tries to give her best irrespective of the composition of the film. Dialogues are mediocre. Photography is average. Stunts are normal. Choreography is bad. Lyrics of pathos song are good.

Analysis: Only plus point of the film is music by MM Srilekha. Major liability to the film is the hero. This film has nothing to offer. You can safely avoid watching this film.
