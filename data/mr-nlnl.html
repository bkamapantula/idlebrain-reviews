Review

Story

This is the story of two neighbors Radha Krishna (Tarun) and Krishna Veni (Arti Agrawal). The fathers of this couple are childhood friends and business partners. Radha Krishna and Krishna Veni derive more pleasure in quarrelling and arguing. Look at this enviable pair, their parents ask them if Radha and Krishna are interested in marrying each other. But Radha and Krishna rule it out as they though its impossible for them to stay at piece without quarrelling. But, in their heart, they have immense latent liking towards each other.

In the process, Radha parents seek out alliances for him. As Radha is meeting up the girl his parents selected, Krishna starts feeling jealous and wants to tell that she loves Krishna. Krishna too loves Radha. But he thinks that she is just a friend to him and has no feelings toward her.

Finally Radha realizes that Krishna loves him. And they are in love! Then story takes the only twist when the pharmaceutical factory owned by their parents is burned into ashes by a evil-minded guy who has enmity towards them. The pharmaceutical factory's insurance was not made because of the treachery done by the manager. They also have an associated chit funds company. A rumor spreads that these guy is diverting funds to the pharmaceutical company. The investors come and beat up the parents of Radha and Krishna. At that time, multimillionaire industrialist Vishwanath comes down and pays up all the money.

Later on it is revealed that the grandson of Vishwanath is silently admiring Krishna and wants to marry her. The parents of Krishna and Radha happily agree for the marriage between Krishna and the grandson of Vishwanath.

Now Radha sacrifices his love and takes the task of getting Krishna married to another guy. Krishna is reluctant to marry the new guy! Will the childhood friends tuned lovers get married to each other or not? Find it out yourself by watching 'Nuvvu Leka Nenu Lenu' on the big screen.

Artists:

Tarun: Tarun is looking very smart in this film. His performance is very apt. He did full justice to what the role has offered him.

Arti Agrawal: Arti is very cute and lovely in 'Nuvvu Leka Nenu Lenu'. She is one of the main assets of the film. She very glamorous and is definitely satisfy all her fans with her 'glamorous' looks.

Sunil: Sunil has done very good job as 'Karri Seenu' in this film. He over shadowed Brahmanandam in this film completely.

Technical Departments:

Story - Dialogues - Screenplay - Direction: Y Kasi Vishwanath

Story: The story of the film is a time tested one and is in accordance with the trend. This movie can be branded as Nuvvu Kavali part-III. Anandam was Nuvve Kavali Part II. The story takes all the expected turns. In Nuvve Kavali, it was first time. Hence the people were curious about what comes next. But after seeing plethora of love based subjects similar to Nuvve Kavali, crowds had become smart to estimate about what comes in the next scene.

Screenplay & Direction: Screenplay of the film is tight in the first half and became very routine and predictable as the climax approached. For the first timer, it's a good effort. Direction of 'Nuvvu Leka Nenu Lenu' is very good. Kasi Vishwanath directed the film like a veteran.

Dialogues: Dialogues in this film are very witty. Kasi Viswanath followed the style of Trivikram in penning naughty one-liners.

Music: Music by RP Patnaik is very impressive. A notable aspect of the songs in the film that all the songs except for last song (Godari Laa Vundi) are situational songs and are well knitted into the storyline. 'Chi Chi' song is good more impressive on the screen. 'Nuvvante Naakistam' and 'Nindu Godari' songs look cool on the screen. The background score is also good. RP used the popular tune of 'Eti Velli Poyindi Manasu' from 'Ninne Pelladatha' for the humming 'Nuvvu Leka Nenu Lenu' humming that comes repeatedly in the first half.

Production: The production values are rich. D Suresh Babu has taken every care ensure that film is shaped up in a proper way without missing entertainment value. The promotion is also done with lot of aggression.

Analysis: This is a feel-good and pleasant film with a story that is time tested and in tune with the current trend of youth films. First half of the film is very entertaining. Second half becomes predictable. But the interest is sustained throughout the film. Its minimum guarantee film that has the potential to become a good hit. We have to wait and see how the audiences like the film in the weeks to come.
